hewoco was founded by Ben Lattimore, a digital communications consultant and front-end designer and developer in late 2013.

Ben brings a clean, contemporary yet colourful (and hopefully playful!) aesthetic to his work.

We look for people, organisations and companies that stand out; that aren't afraid to have opinions and purpose.

We've worked with an author to give their new work a platform it deserves, helped a trade union mobilise its members, and built landing pages for exciting London start-ups.

If you'd like to chat about your project or an idea, do get in touch.
